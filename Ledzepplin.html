Led Zepplin

Led zepplin is one of the few rockbands that almost ruled the pop culture from the late 60s to the 80s. With a heavy, guitar-driven sound, they are cited as one of the progenitors of hard rock and heavy metal. one of their all time best songs "stairway to heaven" entirely broke the existing music label records of pop stars back then and elevated their fame to almost like a god levl fandom. it has been nearly 40 years since they had disbanded, still their legacy lives on in the hearts and souls of the millions of fans who still enjoy their music till this date.
