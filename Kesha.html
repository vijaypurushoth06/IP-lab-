Kesha

Kesha is one of the few female pop stars who broke the music industry during late 2000's. she composed songs which were of the jazz genre. her most popular song "die young" managed to reap about 100 million views on youtube even during late 2014. She was a popular feminist icon who supported and preached for the self sufficiency and financial independance of women. her most popular songs include "tik-tok", "die young", "blow", "women" etc..
